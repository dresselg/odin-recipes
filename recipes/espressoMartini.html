Espresso Martini Recipe

The Espresso Martini

Though modern variations come in many forms, the original Espresso Martini is a simple combination of vodka, espresso, and coffee liqueur.

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces vodka
  • 1/2 ounce coffee liqueur
  • 1 ounce espresso, freshly brewed
  • 1/4 ounce simple syrup
  • Garnish: coffee beans

Instructions

  1. Add vodka, coffee liqueur, espresso and simple syrup to a shaker filled with ice and shake until well-chilled.
  2. Strain into a chilled cocktail glass.
  3. Garnish with 3 coffee beans.
